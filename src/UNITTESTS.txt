HOW TO RUN UNIT TESTS

FROM ANT:

The database tests WILL EMPTY YOUR DATABASE and load the seed-rod.xml file.
To prevent this from happening by accident on the Production system, the
seed-rod.xml IS NOT AUTOMATICALLY installed in ../public/WEB-INF/classes/
You must do this yourself.
